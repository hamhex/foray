What's New

Foray is our new band name. We're going to be transitioning to it over the next little while. It just fits better. We'll always remember and cherish the time we spent as The Riffs, but we have grown as both people and musicians, and we want the band to reflect that. We are really (really!) happy with the name change and looking forward to sharing with you all the new music that's going to come with it. Thanks to everyone who has been with us on our journey so far! You guys are amazing.

  • 5 Musical Misfits

    The members of The Riffs began playing together in elementary school. Formed in Markham, Ontario in 2011 by then pint-sized friends Scott Zhang, Justin Chen, David and Jonathan Matta, The Riffs first appeared onstage with friend and keyboard player Jocelyn Lee in 2014. It wasn't long before they began to play at local shows and events. No longer content with performing covers, the band has recently begun crafting their own style of poignant indie rock, drawing upon a vast (and constantly expanding) array of musical influences.
